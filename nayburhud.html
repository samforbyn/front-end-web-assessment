People's Freeway (Ballpark)


It's a fairly relaxed part of Salt Lake City, with our biggest attraction being Smith's Ballpark. Right down the street from the ballpark, you can find Lucky 13, where you can find a variety of burgers, wraps, sandwiches. It's well rated and has a solid reputation. In this part of town you can expect a diverse community, dogs at the majority of houses, and a (relatively) low crime rate. You can view actual statistcs here.
